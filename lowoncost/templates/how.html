How it Works

Last updated: January 1, 2025

Introduction

We have tried to make the process simple so that anyone can start their journey of affiliate marketing with us.

Create an account with a valid email ID and a unique username. Remember the username is the most important element, as you will be known from that username.

Get started with LowonCost

Just after creating an account, you can start creating new deals with LowonCost.

  • Add a new deal by clicking on the Add New Deal button.
  • Add a valid title, image URL (we do not support image upload), affiliate link, a catchy SEO-friendly description, and other details.
  • Do not upload the URL of a spam website. This may lead to account termination.
  • All details must be correct; else the deals may get rejected in the verification process.
  • Verification sometimes may take a little longer.

After varification

You are ready to go:

  • Your deals will be available on the home page.
  • People can see them and buy products from your link.

Share what people need

We have tried to make a site for people who are deal finders. Only upload valid deals so that you can generate trust among our users and earn more.
