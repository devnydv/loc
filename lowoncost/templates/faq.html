Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about DealsHub

General Questions

What is LowonCost?
LowonCost is your one-stop destination for finding the best deals and discounts across various categories. We curate and verify deals from trusted retailers to help you save money on your purchases.
How do I create an account?
Creating an account is simple! Click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner, fill in simple details. Once done, you can start saving and sharing deals!

Deals & Offers

How do you verify deals?
Our team manually verifies each deal before it's published. We check for authenticity, price accuracy, and terms & conditions to ensure you get genuine savings opportunities.
What if a deal doesn't work?
If you encounter any issues with a deal, please report it using the "Report Issue" button on the deal page. Our team will investigate and update or remove the deal if necessary.

Account & Settings

How do I change my password?
As of now changing password can only be done by contacting the admin with your correct details.
Can I delete my account?
Yes, you can delete your account through Profile > Edit Profile > Delete Account. Please note this action is permanent and cannot be undone. Your account will be gone but deals that you have created may remain for some time.
